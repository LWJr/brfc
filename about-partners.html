Literacy for Children from Birth to 5

  • Monthly literacy activity calendars in Spanish and English for two age groups
  • Additional booklist suggestions and literacy crafts & activities
  • RIF's STEAM themed Multicultural Book Collection is a set of 40 carefully selected titles with accompanying literacy activity sheets for educators, parents and community volunteers. Information about the 2014 STEAM book collection and the free, printable activity sheets
  • Stay Connected- you might like to take the pledge to be a Book Person to stay up to date on our RIF initiatives, resources and other fun and exciting children's literacy information
  • The American Academy of Pediatrics has a great new initiative that ties literacy to health. It’s called Books Build Connections Tool Kit

Total Wellness for Children

  • Our foundation supports the vision of total wellness for all children, including physical and mental health. We also advocate for equal resources to ensure total wellness and health for children.
  • An array of information on everything from healthy behaviors to violence and school shootings aftercare is available on the website of the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry
  • Information to promote mental health and prevent substance abuse is available on the website of Mental Health America
  • Information on research and publications about children and school health-Robert Woods Johnson Foundation
  • Information on education and community based health programs for underserved children-Morehouse School of Medicine
  • Information on Mental Health Initiatives by Pediatricians - American Academy of Pediatrics

Children and the Law

  • For information regarding advocacy, policy, research, training and technical assistance (especially foster care) consult the American Bar Association/Center on Children and the Law
  • Services to at-risk youth and youth in trouble are offered by the Children and the Law Blog
  • A voice for the education of children in foster care can be found at the Legal Center for Foster Care and Education
  • For information on reform of the juvenile justice system and the protection of children in the system consult the Center for Children’s Law and Policy
  • Legal resources for federal and state policies and laws regarding children
  • Advocacy for abused and neglected kids in the child welfare system
